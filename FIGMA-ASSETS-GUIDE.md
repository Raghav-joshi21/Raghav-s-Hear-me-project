# Figma Assets Export Guide

To implement the exact graphics from your Figma design, please export the following assets:

## Steps to Export Assets from Figma:

1. **Open your Figma design**: https://www.figma.com/design/5C2lTBlyKZrcS0amLj8BtA/Untitled?node-id=51-328

2. **Select each element** and export as needed:

### Assets Needed for Communication Page:

#### Left Panel (Deaf Section):

- **Camera Icon** - Export as PNG/SVG (if custom icon)
- **Audio Player Icons** - Play button, speaker icon, waveform graphic
- **Any background graphics or patterns**

#### Right Panel (Hearing Section):

- **Status Indicator Icons** - Status dot, microphone icon
- **Avatar/Sign Language Interpreter** - Image or illustration (if not using emoji)
- **Live Indicator Graphics** - Red dot or live badge
- **Any other visual elements**

#### Shared Elements:

- **Background graphics** (if any patterns/textures)
- **Section header graphics** (if custom styled)
- **Button icons or graphics**
- **Any decorative elements**

## Export Settings:

- **Format**: PNG (for photos/images) or SVG (for icons/illustrations)
- **Size**:
  - Icons: 2x or 3x (e.g., 48px icon â†’ export at 96px or 144px)
  - Images: As needed (match display size or 2x)
- **Naming**: Use descriptive names like:
  - `camera-icon.svg`
  - `audio-play-button.svg`
  - `status-indicator.png`
  - `avatar-sign-language.png`

## After Export:

1. **Save all assets** in the `public/assets/` folder
2. **Share the exported files** or upload them to Cloudinary (like your logo)
3. **I'll update the code** to use these assets

Alternatively, if you prefer:

- Upload to Cloudinary and share the URLs
- Or describe what graphics are used and I can suggest alternatives
